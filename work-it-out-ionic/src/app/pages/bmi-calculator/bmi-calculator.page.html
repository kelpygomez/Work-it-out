Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It is commonly used to classify individuals into different weight categories, such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity.

A BMI calculator helps you determine your BMI by inputting your weight and height. Once calculated, your BMI can give you an indication of your overall health status.

Here's how BMI categories are typically interpreted:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 or greater

It's important to note that while BMI is a useful screening tool, it may not be accurate for all individuals, particularly those with a high muscle mass. It's always best to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized health advice.
